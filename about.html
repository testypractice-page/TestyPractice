About the platform & creator

Testy is a clean, simple and completely free MCQ practice platform specially made for CA Foundation students.

No subscriptions. No paid test series. No limits. Just open → choose chapter → practice.

A Note for Users

Testy is designed for consistency and clarity. You can use it for:

  • Daily MCQ practice
  • Chapter-wise preparation
  • RTP/MTP solving
  • Instant checking & review
  • Downloading your results as reports

Show up every day - even 20 minutes - and your performance will improve automatically.
